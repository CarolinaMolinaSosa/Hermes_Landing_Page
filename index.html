About Us

We are passionate students of Holberton Uruguay, for our final project we decided to use what we learned in the curriculum and more. We implemented artificial intelligence and machine learning to create a solution dedicated to marketing campaigns. We aim to improve the time spent on manual surveys through the recognition of expressions. This is a Portfolio Project for Holberton School.
